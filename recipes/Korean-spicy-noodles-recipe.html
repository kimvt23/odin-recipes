Korean spicy noodles recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 oz somyeon (thin wheat noodles) or buckwheat/soba noodles
  • cucumber, kimchi, boiled egg, crushed sesame seeds, and/or any other toppings of choice optional, for garnish
    • Sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar or rice vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Directions

Step 1 Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl and set aside.

Step 2 Cook noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse the noodles in ice-cold water, rubbing them with your hands to remove excess starch. Drain well and place in a bowl.

Step 3 Add the sauce over the noodles and mix together until the noodles are evenly coated. Garnish and enjoy!
